Air Fryer Weiner Schnitzel

Description

Rich, tender, and juicy wiener schnitzel in the air fryer. So tender you can cut it with a fork.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound veal, scallopini cut
  • 2T lemon juice
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4c all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1T chopped fresh parsley
  • 1c panko bread crumbs
  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 C)
  2. Place veal on a clean work surface and sprinkle with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place flour in a flat dish. Beat egg and parsley together in a second dish. Place breadcrumbs in a thir dish.
  4. Dredge each veal cutlet first in flour, then in egg mixture, followed by breadcrumbs. Press down so that breadcrumbs adhere.
  5. Spray the basket/tray of air fryer with nonstick cooking spray. Place breaded veal cutlets into basket/tray, making sure not to overcrowd. Spray tops of cutlets with nonstick cooking spray.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes. Flip each cutlet and spray and dry spots with nonstick cooking spray. Cook an additional 5 minutes.
  7. Repeat steps 4 and 5 with any remaining veal
  8. Serve with lemon wedges
