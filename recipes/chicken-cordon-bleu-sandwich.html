Grilled Chicken Cordon Bleu Sandwiches

Description

These hot sandwiches are quick and easy, not to mention delicious. Serve with salad or soup for a full meal

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2T butter, softened
  • 1T sour cream
  • 2 slices swiss cheese
  • 1 thick slice deli chicken breast
  • 1 slice deli ham

Directions

  1. Spread sour cream on inside of bread slices
  2. Layer sandwich as follows: Bread slice, cheese, chikcen, ham, 2nd cheese slice, 2nd bread slice
  3. Spread butter on outside of bread slices
  4. Heat a small skillet over medium-high heat
  5. Grill sandwich until meat is heated through and cheese has melted
